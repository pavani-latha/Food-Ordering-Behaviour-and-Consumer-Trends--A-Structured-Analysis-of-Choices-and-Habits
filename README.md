# 🍽️ Food Delivery Analytics Dashboard | Tableau

An interactive **Tableau Dashboard** built to analyze food delivery data by exploring customer behavior, meal preferences, city-wise order trends, revenue contribution, and delivery performance.

🔗 **Live Dashboard:**  
https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/pavani.latha.pediredla/viz/FoodOrderStory_17837765627210/Story1

---

# 📌 Project Overview

This Tableau dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of a food delivery business by visualizing key operational and customer metrics.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to:

- Monitor overall order performance
- Compare customer meal preferences
- Analyze revenue generated by different meal types
- Identify high-performing cities
- Understand customer ordering patterns
- Evaluate delivery performance
- Support business decision-making through interactive visualizations

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze food ordering trends across cities.
- Understand customer meal preferences.
- Compare revenue generated by different meal types.
- Identify top-performing cities based on order volume.
- Analyze customer demographics and restaurant preferences.
- Monitor delivery efficiency.

# 📊 Dashboard Components

## 1️⃣ Key Performance Metrics

Displays overall business KPIs such as:

- Total Orders
- Delivery Fee
- Order Value
- Average Rating

---

## 2️⃣ Company Type & Meal Type Orders

Analyzes customer ordering patterns across different company types:

- Alone
- Family
- Friends
- Partner

Meal Types:

- Breakfast
- Lunch
- Dinner
- Snacks

---

## 3️⃣ City vs Cuisine Analysis

Compares customer preferences for different cuisines across major cities.

Cities Included:

- Mumbai
- Bangalore
- Delhi
- Hyderabad
- Chandigarh
- Pune

Cuisine Categories:

- Biryani
- Chinese
- Desserts
- Fast Food
- North Indian
- South Indian

---

## 4️⃣ Top 5 Cities by Order Volume

Ranks cities based on total order count.

Helps identify:

- Highest performing markets
- Customer demand distribution

---

## 5️⃣ Cuisine Preference Distribution

Pie Chart showing customer preference percentages across various cuisines.

Provides quick insight into the most popular food categories.

---

## 6️⃣ Orders by Age Group & Restaurant Type

Analyzes ordering behavior among customer age groups.

Age Groups:

- Gen Z
- Millennials
- Adults
- Children

Useful for targeted marketing strategies.

---

## 7️⃣ Revenue Contribution by Meal Type

Compares revenue generated from:

- Breakfast
- Lunch
- Dinner
- Snacks

Helps identify the highest revenue-generating meal category.

---

## 8️⃣ Delivery Time Distribution

Visualizes delivery performance using delivery time metrics.

Supports operational efficiency analysis.
